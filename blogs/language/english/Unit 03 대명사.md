## 개요

대명사는 **격(case)**과 **수 일치**가 핵심. 인칭 대명사 격 변화표만 정확히 외우면 토익 대명사 문제의 절반은 풀린다.

## 핵심 문법

### 1. 인칭대명사 격 변화

| 주격 | 소유격 | 목적격 | 소유대명사 | 재귀대명사 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| I | my | me | mine | myself |
| you | your | you | yours | yourself / yourselves |
| he | his | him | his | himself |
| she | her | her | hers | herself |
| it | its | it | — | itself |
| we | our | us | ours | ourselves |
| they | their | them | theirs | themselves |

- 주격: 주어 자리 (*She will lead the project.*)
- 소유격: 명사 앞 (*her project*)
- 목적격: 동사·전치사 뒤 (*assigned to him*)
- 소유대명사 = 소유격 + 명사 (*the office is hers*)

### 2. 재귀대명사 (-self / -selves)

- **재귀 용법**: 주어 = 목적어일 때 (*She introduced herself.*)
- **강조 용법**: 의미 강조, 생략 가능 (*The CEO himself attended.*)
- **관용 표현**: *by oneself* (혼자), *for oneself* (혼자 힘으로)

### 3. 부정대명사

- **some / any**: 긍정문 some / 부정·의문문 any
- **each / every + 단수 명사 + 단수 동사**: *Each employee has a desk.*
- **all / most / some + of + the/소유격 + 명사** (수 일치는 뒤 명사에 따름)
- **one / another / others / the others**: *One is here, the others are out.*
- **either / neither**: 둘 중 하나 / 둘 다 아닌

### 4. 지시대명사

- **this / these**: 가까운 것
- **that / those**: 먼 것
- *the price of A is higher than **that** of B.* (반복 명사 대신 that/those)

## TOEIC 빈출 패턴

- `The manager praised _____ for the work.` → him/her/them (목적격)
- `_____ office is on the 5th floor.` → His/Her/Their (소유격)
- `Mr. Kim, along with his team, _____ the report.` → submitted (수식어 무시, 주어는 Mr. Kim)
- `Each of the employees _____ assigned a desk.` → was/is (each = 단수)

## 예문

1. The CEO will introduce **himself** at the meeting.
2. **Each** of the candidates has a unique background.
3. The price of our product is lower than **that** of competitors.
4. Please submit **your** report to **me** by tomorrow.

## 연습 문제

1. Mr. Lee submitted _____ report ahead of the deadline.
   (A) he  (B) his  (C) him  (D) himself

2. All applicants should bring _____ identification documents.
   (A) they  (B) their  (C) them  (D) theirs

3. The director _____ approved the new policy.
   (A) she  (B) her  (C) hers  (D) herself

**정답**

1. (B) his — 명사 report 앞 소유격
2. (B) their — 명사 documents 앞 소유격
3. (D) herself — 주어 강조 (재귀대명사 강조 용법)
