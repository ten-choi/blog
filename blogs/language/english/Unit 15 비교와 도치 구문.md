## 개요

비교 구문은 **형태 매칭 (as ~ as, more ~ than, the ~est)**과 **관용 표현**이 핵심. 도치는 부정어·only가 문장 앞에 나올 때의 **조동사 + 주어** 어순이 가장 자주 출제된다.

## 핵심 문법

### 1. 비교의 3단계

- **원급**: as + 형용사/부사 원형 + as
- **비교급**: 형용사/부사 + -er + than 또는 more + 원형 + than
- **최상급**: the + 형용사/부사 + -est 또는 the + most + 원형

### 2. 원급 비교 (as ~ as)

- 긍정: *as fast as*
- 부정: *not as/so fast as*
- 배수: *twice / three times + as ~ as* (— *twice as expensive as*)

### 3. 비교급 (~er / more ~)

- *higher than*, *more efficient than*
- 비교급 강조 부사: **much, far, even, still, a lot, considerably** (very는 비교급 강조 X)
- 비교급 + and + 비교급: 점점 더 ~ (*more and more important*)
- the + 비교급, the + 비교급: ~할수록 더 ~ (*The more you practice, the better you get.*)

### 4. 최상급

- the + -est / the most + 원형
- 범위: *of all*, *in the company*, *that I have ever seen*
- one of the + 최상급 + 복수 명사: *one of the most efficient processes*

### 5. 비교 구문 관용 표현

- **as ~ as possible** (가능한 한 ~하게)
- **no longer** (더 이상 ~ 아닌)
- **more than ever** (그 어느 때보다)
- **other than** (~ 이외에)
- **rather than** (~보다는 차라리)
- **prefer A to B** (B보다 A를 선호)
- **superior to / inferior to** (~보다 우수한/열등한 — than X)

### 6. 도치 구문 (주어와 (조)동사 어순 바뀜)

#### (1) 부정어가 문장 앞에 올 때

부정어: not, no, never, hardly, scarcely, rarely, seldom, little, nor, neither

- *Never **have I seen** such dedication.*
- *Hardly **had he arrived** when the meeting started.*

#### (2) only가 문장 앞에 올 때

- *Only after the deadline **did we realize** the mistake.*
- *Only by working together **can we** succeed.*

#### (3) 조건절 if 생략 도치

- *Had I known sooner, I would have acted.* (= If I had known...)
- *Were he available, he would attend.* (= If he were available...)
- *Should you need help, contact me.* (= If you should need...)

#### (4) so / neither + 조동사 + 주어

- *I'm tired. — **So am I**.*
- *She doesn't smoke. — **Neither do I**.*

#### (5) 장소 부사구 앞 도치

- *On the desk **was** a stack of reports.*
- *Among the candidates **was** Ms. Lee.*

## TOEIC 빈출 패턴

- `the + 비교급 + 주어 + 동사, the + 비교급 + 주어 + 동사`
- `as + 원급 + as possible`
- `superior / inferior + to` (not than)
- `Not only + 조동사 + 주어 + 동사, but also ~`
- `Should + 주어 + 동사원형, 주어 + 조동사 + 동사원형` (조건 도치)

## 예문

1. The new system is **far more efficient than** the previous one.
2. Please respond **as soon as possible**.
3. **The more** we invest, **the higher** the return.
4. **Not only** did sales increase, **but** profits also rose.

## 연습 문제

1. Our product is _____ than any other in the market.
   (A) reliable  (B) more reliable  (C) most reliable  (D) the most reliable

2. _____ I known about the change, I would have prepared.
   (A) If  (B) Had  (C) Have  (D) Should

3. Our service is far _____ to that of competitors.
   (A) superior  (B) better  (C) higher  (D) more

**정답**

1. (B) more reliable — than과 결합, 비교급
2. (B) Had — if 생략 도치 (Had I known = If I had known)
3. (A) superior — superior to (than과 결합 불가)
