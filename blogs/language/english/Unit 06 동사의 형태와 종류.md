## 개요

동사는 **자동사/타동사** 구분과 **5형식별 동사**가 핵심. 자동사인데 목적어를 받으려고 전치사를 빠뜨리거나, 타동사에 불필요한 전치사를 붙이는 실수가 토익 단골이다.

## 핵심 문법

### 1. 동사의 5가지 형태

- 원형: work
- 3인칭 단수 현재: works
- 과거: worked
- 과거분사: worked
- 현재분사 / 동명사: working

### 2. 자동사 vs 타동사

- **자동사 (intransitive)**: 목적어 X. 전치사 동반.
- **타동사 (transitive)**: 목적어 직접.

**자동사 + 전치사** 묶음 (토익 단골):

| 묶음 | 의미 |
|---|---|
| arrive **at / in** | 도착하다 |
| respond **to** | 응답하다 |
| participate **in** | 참여하다 |
| comply **with** | 준수하다 |
| account **for** | 설명하다, 차지하다 |
| consist **of** | 구성되다 |
| depend **on** | 의존하다 |
| refrain **from** | 삼가다 |

**타동사 — 전치사 붙이면 틀림**:

- **discuss** (○ discuss the issue / ✗ discuss about)
- **attend** (○ attend the meeting / ✗ attend to the meeting — "to"는 다른 뜻)
- **approach** (○ approach the problem / ✗ approach to)
- **enter** (○ enter the building / ✗ enter into the building)
- **address** (○ address the concern)
- **mention** (○ mention the change)

### 3. 혼동 동사 (rise/raise 등)

| 자동사 | 타동사 |
|---|---|
| rise — rose — risen (오르다) | raise — raised — raised (~을 올리다) |
| lie — lay — lain (눕다) | lay — laid — laid (~을 놓다) |
| sit — sat — sat (앉다) | seat — seated — seated (~을 앉히다) |

### 4. 4형식·5형식 동사

**4형식 (S + V + IO + DO)**: give, send, offer, show, tell, bring, lend

- *They offered me the position.* = They offered the position **to** me.

**5형식 (S + V + O + OC)**:
- 목적격 보어가 형용사: find/keep/leave + O + 형용사 — *Keep the door open.*
- 목적격 보어가 to부정사: ask/allow/advise/encourage/expect/require + O + to V
- 목적격 보어가 동사원형: make/let/have + O + V원형 (사역동사)

## TOEIC 빈출 패턴

- `participate _____ the program` → in (자동사 + 전치사)
- `discuss _____ the proposal` → 빈칸 X (타동사, 전치사 불필요)
- `Please _____ the meeting tomorrow.` → attend (타동사)
- `The committee will _____ the policy.` → review / discuss / approve (타동사)

## 예문

1. All employees must **comply with** the safety regulations.
2. The board will **discuss** the proposal at the next meeting.
3. Sales have **risen** by 15% this year.
4. The manager **asked** us **to submit** the report by Friday.

## 연습 문제

1. The conference will _____ over 500 participants.
   (A) attract  (B) attract to  (C) attract for  (D) attract from

2. All staff must _____ in the safety training.
   (A) attend  (B) participate  (C) join in  (D) participate in (둘 답)
   → 정답 보기는 (D) participate in

3. The director _____ everyone to submit reports by Friday.
   (A) made  (B) asked  (C) had  (D) let

**정답**

1. (A) attract — attract는 타동사라 전치사 불필요
2. (D) participate in — participate는 자동사, 전치사 in 동반
3. (B) asked — ask + 목적어 + to부정사 (make/have/let은 동사원형 받음)
