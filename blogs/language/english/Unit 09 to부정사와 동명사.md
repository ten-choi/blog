## 개요

`to + 동사원형`은 **명사/형용사/부사** 역할을 다 하고, `동사-ing(동명사)`는 **명사 역할**만. 토익에서는 **어떤 동사가 to V를 받고 어떤 동사가 -ing을 받는지** 외워두면 절반은 끝난다.

## 핵심 문법

### 1. to부정사의 3가지 역할

- **명사적**: *To finish the report* takes time. / I want **to leave**.
- **형용사적**: I have a report **to submit**. (앞 명사 수식)
- **부사적**: She came **to discuss** the budget. (목적: ~하기 위해)

### 2. 동명사 (V-ing)의 역할

- 주어: *Submitting* the form is required.
- 목적어: I enjoy *working* here.
- 보어: My job is *managing* the team.
- 전치사 목적어: She is good at *negotiating*. (전치사 뒤 동명사 — to V ✗)

### 3. to V만 받는 동사 (목표·미래 지향)

want, decide, plan, hope, expect, wish, agree, refuse, promise, manage, fail, intend, choose, offer, afford, learn

- *We decided **to launch** the product.*

### 4. -ing만 받는 동사 (현재·진행 지향)

enjoy, finish, avoid, consider, suggest, recommend, postpone, mind, give up, deny, keep, practice, admit

- *She suggested **postponing** the meeting.*

### 5. 둘 다 받지만 의미 다른 동사

| 동사 | + to V | + -ing |
|---|---|---|
| remember | (앞으로) ~할 것을 기억 | (과거에) ~했던 것을 기억 |
| forget | (앞으로) ~할 것을 잊다 | (과거에) ~했던 것을 잊다 |
| try | ~하려고 노력하다 | 시험삼아 ~해보다 |
| stop | ~하기 위해 멈추다 | ~하는 것을 그만두다 |

### 6. 동명사 관용 표현

- be **used to** -ing (~하는 데 익숙하다)
- look forward to -ing
- be committed/dedicated to -ing
- contribute to -ing
- in addition to -ing
- have difficulty (in) -ing
- spend + 시간/돈 + -ing

(`to`가 전치사인 경우 뒤에 동명사 — 토익 함정 단골)

### 7. 5형식 — 목적격 보어로 to V

ask, allow, encourage, advise, expect, require, invite, remind + O + **to V**

- *The manager **asked** us **to submit** the report.*

## TOEIC 빈출 패턴

- `decided / plan / hope + _____` → to V
- `enjoy / finish / consider + _____` → V-ing
- `look forward to / be committed to + _____` → V-ing
- `ask / require / encourage + 목적어 + _____` → to V
- `It is important / necessary + _____` → to V

## 예문

1. We are committed **to providing** excellent service.
2. The board decided **to delay** the launch.
3. The CEO encouraged employees **to share** their ideas.
4. She finished **preparing** the presentation late at night.

## 연습 문제

1. The committee agreed _____ the meeting until next week.
   (A) postpone  (B) postponing  (C) to postpone  (D) postponed

2. We look forward to _____ you at the event.
   (A) see  (B) seeing  (C) seen  (D) be seeing

3. The manager asked all employees _____ the survey.
   (A) complete  (B) completing  (C) to complete  (D) completed

**정답**

1. (C) to postpone — agree + to V
2. (B) seeing — look forward **to** + -ing (to는 전치사)
3. (C) to complete — ask + O + to V
