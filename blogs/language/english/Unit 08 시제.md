## 개요

시제는 **시간 단서 부사**가 답을 알려주는 경우가 많다. *currently*, *recently*, *next week*, *since 2020* 같은 표현이 보이면 일치하는 시제를 고른다.

## 핵심 문법

### 1. 12시제 정리

| | 단순 | 진행 | 완료 | 완료진행 |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 현재 | works | is working | has worked | has been working |
| 과거 | worked | was working | had worked | had been working |
| 미래 | will work | will be working | will have worked | will have been working |

### 2. 시제별 시간 단서

| 시제 | 단서 부사 |
|---|---|
| 현재 | usually, always, often, every day, generally |
| 현재진행 | now, currently, at the moment, right now |
| 과거 | yesterday, last week, ago, in 2020 |
| 현재완료 | already, just, yet, recently, lately, since, for, so far, over the past N years |
| 과거완료 | by the time + 과거, before/after + 과거 |
| 미래 | tomorrow, next week, soon, in the near future |
| 미래완료 | by + 미래 시점 (by next year, by 2030) |

### 3. 현재완료 vs 과거

- **현재완료**: 과거에 일어난 일이 지금에 영향. 구체적 과거 시점 부사 X.
  - *She has worked here for five years.*
- **과거**: 끝난 일. 명확한 과거 시점 부사 O.
  - *She worked here in 2020.*

### 4. 시간·조건 부사절: 미래는 현재로

- 시간 부사절(when, before, after, until, as soon as) + 조건 부사절(if, unless)
- 의미상 미래라도 **현재 시제** 사용
  - *When the report **arrives**, we will review it.* (○)
  - *When the report will arrive, we will review it.* (✗)

### 5. 과거완료의 쓰임

- 과거의 두 사건 중 **더 먼저** 일어난 일
- *By the time the manager arrived, the meeting **had started**.*

### 6. 미래완료

- 미래 어느 시점까지의 완료
- *By next year, she **will have worked** here for 10 years.*

## TOEIC 빈출 패턴

- `over the past N years / since 2020` → 현재완료 (has worked)
- `currently / at the moment` → 현재진행 (is working)
- `by next month` → 미래완료 (will have completed)
- `When + 주어 + 현재시제, 주어 + will V` → if/when절 현재형

## 예문

1. We **have been working** on this project for six months.
2. The CEO **is currently meeting** with the board.
3. By the time you receive this, I **will have left** the office.
4. The shipment **arrived** last Tuesday.

## 연습 문제

1. The company _____ its sales over the past three years.
   (A) doubles  (B) doubled  (C) has doubled  (D) will double

2. We will inform you as soon as the package _____.
   (A) arrives  (B) will arrive  (C) arrived  (D) has arrived

3. By next year, the firm _____ in business for 50 years.
   (A) is  (B) has been  (C) was  (D) will have been

**정답**

1. (C) has doubled — over the past three years → 현재완료
2. (A) arrives — 시간 부사절(as soon as) 미래는 현재형
3. (D) will have been — by + 미래 시점 → 미래완료
