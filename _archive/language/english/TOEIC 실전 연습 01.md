## 개요

토익 빈출 어휘 12개를 Part 5·6·7 형식으로 점검한다. 단어 → 단문 빈칸 → 지문 빈칸 → 독해 순서로 난이도를 올리며, 마지막에 정답·해설을 모아둔다.

## 빈출 어휘

| 단어 | 품사·의미 | 예문 |
|---|---|---|
| briefly | adv. 간단히, 잠깐 | *Could you briefly summarize the report before the meeting starts?* |
| conduct | n. 행동 / v. 수행하다, 지도하다 | *The professor will conduct the experiment in the lab next week.* |
| discrimination | n. 구별, 차별 | *The new law prohibits discrimination in hiring based on age.* |
| acquire | v. 획득하다, 습득하다 | *She acquired a strong command of Japanese after two years in Tokyo.* |
| confidential | adj. 기밀의 | *Please keep these documents confidential and do not share them outside the team.* |
| evaluation | n. 평가 | *The team will receive a performance evaluation at the end of the quarter.* |
| suspect | n. 용의자 / v. 의심하다 | *The police questioned the main suspect for several hours.* |
| factor | n. 요소, 요인 | *Cost was the main factor in our decision.* |
| consent | n. 동의 / v. 동의하다 | *The patient signed a form to give consent for the surgery.* |
| ensure | v. 보장하다, 확실히 하다 | *Please ensure that all the doors are locked before you leave.* |
| adequate | adj. 충분한, 적절한 | *We don't have adequate funding to finish the project this year.* |
| urgency | n. 긴급(성) | *The situation called for a sense of urgency from the whole team.* |

## Part 5 — 단문 빈칸

1. The manager asked the intern to _____ explain the new policy before the meeting ended.
   (A) brief  (B) briefly  (C) briefness  (D) briefing

2. All client records must remain strictly _____ at all times.
   (A) confide  (B) confidence  (C) confidential  (D) confidentially

3. The HR director will _____ a survey to measure employee satisfaction next month.
   (A) conduct  (B) conduction  (C) conducted  (D) conductor

4. Rising fuel prices were the main _____ behind the decline in quarterly profits.
   (A) factor  (B) factors  (C) factored  (D) factoring

5. The board emphasized the _____ of finalizing the contract before the deadline.
   (A) urgent  (B) urgency  (C) urgently  (D) urge

6. The company failed to provide _____ training for the new safety equipment.
   (A) adequate  (B) adequacy  (C) adequately  (D) adequateness

7. Written _____ from the client is required before any design changes are made.
   (A) consent  (B) consented  (C) consenting  (D) consents

8. Please _____ that all attendees have signed the visitor log.
   (A) insure  (B) assure  (C) ensure  (D) reassure

9. After the merger, the firm quickly _____ three smaller competitors in the region.
   (A) acquires  (B) acquired  (C) acquiring  (D) acquisition

10. The annual performance _____ will be conducted online this year.
    (A) evaluate  (B) evaluated  (C) evaluation  (D) evaluative

## Part 6 — 장문 빈칸

다음은 사내 메모다.

> **MEMO**
> To: All Staff
> From: HR Department
> Subject: Year-End Performance Review
>
> As the fiscal year approaches its end, we will soon begin the annual performance ---**(11)**---. Each manager is expected to ---**(12)**--- a one-on-one meeting with every team member by December 15. All discussion contents must be treated as ---**(13)**--- and should not be shared with anyone outside the review process. Please ---**(14)**--- that you submit your self-assessment form at least two business days before your scheduled meeting.

11. (A) evaluate  (B) evaluation  (C) evaluative  (D) evaluator
12. (A) acquire  (B) ensure  (C) conduct  (D) suspect
13. (A) confidence  (B) confidential  (C) confidentially  (D) confidant
14. (A) ensure  (B) ensures  (C) ensured  (D) ensuring

## Part 7 — 독해

다음 기사를 읽고 물음에 답하시오.

> Lumen Analytics, a mid-sized data firm based in Boston, announced on Monday that it has **acquired** Halcyon Metrics, a smaller competitor specializing in retail analytics. While the financial terms remain **confidential**, sources **briefly** familiar with the deal estimate the transaction at roughly $40 million.
>
> The combined company will **conduct** a full review of overlapping product lines over the next quarter. Lumen's CEO, Diana Park, said in a statement that cost synergy was not the primary **factor** behind the move. "Our goal is to acquire the engineering talent and the client base — that's where the real value sits," she said. "We will **ensure** that all existing Halcyon contracts are honored without disruption."
>
> Industry analysts noted a sense of **urgency** in the deal, as several rival firms had reportedly been negotiating with Halcyon in recent weeks.

15. What is the main purpose of the article?
    (A) To announce a corporate merger
    (B) To report on a financial scandal
    (C) To introduce a new product line
    (D) To compare retail analytics tools

16. According to Diana Park, why did Lumen pursue the deal?
    (A) To reduce overlapping costs
    (B) To gain engineers and clients
    (C) To enter the Boston market
    (D) To replace its existing CEO

17. The word "factor" in paragraph 2 is closest in meaning to
    (A) result  (B) reason  (C) feature  (D) employee

18. What is implied about Halcyon Metrics?
    (A) It will be shut down immediately.
    (B) Other companies were also interested in it.
    (C) Its clients will be transferred to a third party.
    (D) Its engineers have refused to join Lumen.

## 정답 및 해설

**Part 5**

1. (B) briefly — 동사 *explain*을 수식하는 자리, 부사.
2. (C) confidential — be동사 뒤 보어 자리, 형용사.
3. (A) conduct — 조동사 *will* 뒤 동사원형.
4. (A) factor — 정관사 *the* 뒤 단수명사 자리, 뒤에 단수 동사 *was*.
5. (B) urgency — 정관사 *the* 뒤 명사 자리.
6. (A) adequate — 명사 *training* 앞 형용사 자리.
7. (A) consent — 형용사 *Written* 뒤 명사 자리.
8. (C) ensure — "확실히 하다(that절)". *insure*는 보험, *assure*는 사람을 안심시킬 때 사용.
9. (B) acquired — 시간 부사 *quickly* + 과거 시제 흐름, 주어 *firm* 단수.
10. (C) evaluation — 명사 *performance* 뒤에 명사가 와서 복합명사를 이룬다.

**Part 6**

11. (B) evaluation — 정관사 *the* + 명사 *performance* 뒤 복합명사 자리.
12. (C) conduct — *conduct a meeting* (회의를 진행하다) 연어.
13. (B) confidential — *be treated as* 뒤 보어 자리, 형용사.
14. (A) ensure — *Please* 뒤 동사원형, 명령문.

**Part 7**

15. (A) — 첫 문단이 인수 사실을 알리는 내용이다.
16. (B) — Park CEO가 "engineering talent and client base"가 진짜 가치라고 언급.
17. (B) reason — *factor behind the move*는 "결정의 이유". *reason*이 가장 가깝다.
18. (B) — 마지막 문단에서 "several rival firms had reportedly been negotiating with Halcyon"이라고 언급.
